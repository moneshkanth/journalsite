IJDTS (International Journal of Developments in Technology and Science) is an international peer-reviewed, open access and online journal. It is written as IJDTS in short form. (ISSN: 2581-9119). The aim and scope of the journal is to provide a high quality and an important reference for the advancement of research results that support high-level learning to teaching and research communities in the fields of Technology and Science. It covers manufacturing, technology developments, software updates, security issues and solutions, technical reviews, shot communications, applications, innovative approaches and research of Technology and Science.The Journal will provide an excellent international forum for sharing knowledge and results in Emerging Technological Innovations in Science, Engineering and Technology. The Journal creates a scope or gap to think the academic and researchers towards the innovations in the latest trends..

Important Dates:
Paper Submission : Round the clock
Acknowledgment of the Paper : Within 2 Day
Initial Notification : Within 10 Days
Final Notification : Within 20 Days
Online Publication : Within 30 Days
